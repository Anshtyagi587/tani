Stay Safe During Your Rides

Your safety is our priority. Follow these essential tips to ensure a secure journey every time.

Essential Safety Tips

Share Your Ride

Always share your ride details with a trusted person.

Use Emergency Features

Utilize the app’s built-in emergency features for quick help.

Avoid Front Seat

Sit in the back seat when traveling alone for added safety.

Save Emergency Contacts

Keep emergency numbers on speed dial for quick access.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels off, get out of the vehicle safely.

Keep Phone Charged

Ensure your phone is fully charged before the ride.
